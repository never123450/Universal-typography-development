Major construction work was completed on September 27, 2016 on the Hong Kong Zhuhai Macao Bridge. The bridge, which connects Hong Kong to the east and Zhuhai and Macao to the west, has a span of 55 kilometers. Its cost exceeds 72 billion yuan (US$10.70 billion). When it’s completed and open to traffic,which is scheduled at the end of next year, it will become the world’s longest cross-sea bridge.
